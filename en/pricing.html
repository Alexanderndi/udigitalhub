What is media budget?

Media budget is what you pay to publishers (Google, Microsoft, Facebook, etc.) to appear on their channels. Most often you pay per clicks on your ads.

U-digitalhub Pricing Explained

All pricing packages refer to the management of your ads through udigitalhub. Besides your monthly subscription package, you will have to pay your media budget on different ad platforms.

Ad/Campaign Duration

The duration & reach of each campaign will depend on your media budget. With udigitalhub, you always stay in control and set the media (advertising) budget you feel happy with.

Pricing FAQ

What does the Free Plan include?
U-digitalhub’s Free Plan includes one campaign creation from a selected advertisement network. You can choose Google Ads, Microsoft Ads or Facebook Ads to start creating your free campaign.
Is your free plan really "Free"?
Yes. Our free plan is completely free if you connect your ad accounts. However, you will have to pay your advertising budget on the different ad channels (Google, Facebook or Microsoft).
Why do you ask for a payment, if it is a free service?
If you don't have existing ad accounts, U-digitalhub will create them for you automatically. In this scenario, you will have to credit your U-digitalhub account with your advertising budget. It will then be used to pay your clicks on the different channels (Google, Facebook & Microsoft).
Do you guarantee sales?
Online success depends on many different factors such as your product or service, market, pricing, website responsiveness, etc. Providing a sales guarantee is therefore impossible. What we can guarantee is best-in-class marketing offering high-quality traffic, which will lead to sales if other factors standards are met.
Are there any cost if I do not connect my ad accounts?
Yes. We are charging a service fee of 10% of your advertising budget if you ask U-digitalhub to create all of your ad accounts. Having only your U-digitalhub account to handle all your ads is a huge time-saver & offers better cross-channel results in the long term.
How much should I spend monthly to get good results?
Defining a single monthly budget is not possible. Every business has different advertising goals and is competing in different markets. What we can advice from the experience of our thousands of clients, is to start with a minimum of 150€/month. Lower budgets won't allow any optimisation, nor leave space & time for notifiable results. Keep in mind that advertising budgets can be adjusted at any time. Many customers are starting small and increase their ad budget over time when they start seeing results.
Will I be charged automatically?
Only if you activate auto payments. U-digitalhub will then charge you automatically when you U-digitalhub balance is reaching zero.
Can I add credits manually every time?
Yes, by default you need to add your U-digitalhub credits (advertising budget) manually. Once you feel comfortable, you can activate auto-payments, which will automatically credit your U-digitalhub account when your credits (advertising budget) reach zero.
How can I stop U-digitalhub payments?
You can downgrade your plan at any moment & get back to our free plan. U-digitalhub credits won't be charged if auto-payment is disabled. We care about customer satisfaction and trust. So you can always contact us at support@udigitalhub.ga if you have any other question.
Can I get refunded if I'm not convinced about the service?
Premium plans payments can be refunded within 15 days after selection. You cannot ask for a refund of used U-digitalhub credits, as they were already consumed to show your ads to potential clients.
