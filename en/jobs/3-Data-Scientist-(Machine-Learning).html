Data Scientist (Machine Learning)

Do you want to help thousands of businesses to grow to new heights? Do you want to become an expert in natural language processing?

 

Data Scientist / Machine Learning

We are looking for a Digital Marketing Consultant in online marketing with an entrepreneurial mindset to join our growing startup in Nigeria. If you like challenges, creativity and are result-driven, then read on!

 

About us

At udigitalhub, we optimize online advertising through Experts & Technology. We are a digital startup founded by a team of Digital marketers, software developers and data scientists. We work directly with the Google office in Nigeria to develop impactful tools helping agencies & SMB’s around the world. Automation, technology and high performance consultancy services are helping our customers to achieve online visibility in a smart and transparent way.

 

Your tasks

 

  • Language processing: Prediction of unprecedented results to minimize waste resulting from suboptimal initial conditions
  • Language generation: Generation of sentences to improve the attractiveness of advertisements
  • Work closely with the founders to boost the company to the next level
  • Provide analysis, presentations and valuable actionables for the company and our clients

 

Your profile

 

  • You have a Bachelor Degree, Master Degree or equivalent in Machine Learning, Applied Mathematics, Information Theory or Equivalent
  • Autonomous worker and able to learn new things on the go
  • You are ambitious and hardworking. You are looking for new challenge and have an entrepreneurial mindset.

 

Your skills

  • Familiarity with main machine learning techniques
  • Capacity to analyse and address the customer’s needs
  • Capacity to design innovative models
  • Knowledge of Python and/or Matlab
  • Experience in Google Adwords and other advertising platforms is a plus

 

Our offer

  • A diverse and innovative work with responsibilities
  • An exciting job in a successful startup based in Nigeria (Abuja)
  • Possibility to play an important role in a fast-growing company
  • An attractive salary based on your experience and profile
  • Status : permanent contract (CDI)
  • Full time


If interested, please send your resume to jobs@udigitalhub.ga
